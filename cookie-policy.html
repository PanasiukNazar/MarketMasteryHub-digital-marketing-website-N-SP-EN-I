Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website dedicated to digital marketing training. These tools help us enhance your experience, improve our content, and analyze usage patterns to provide better learning solutions.

I. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, and layout) over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them whenever you return to the site.

II. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance the performance of our digital marketing training platform. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your learning preferences and session details.
  • Understand how users engage with our content.
  • Improve the functionality and usability of our website.
  • Measure the effectiveness of our digital campaigns.

III. Types of Cookies We Use

We may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic site functionality such as navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data to help us understand how visitors interact with our training content.
  • Functionality Cookies: Allow us to remember choices you make to provide more personalized features.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant content based on your browsing behavior and engagement.

IV. Third-Party Cookies

We may work with trusted third-party providers who use cookies to support our educational content delivery and analytics. These third parties are not permitted to use your data for unrelated purposes.
