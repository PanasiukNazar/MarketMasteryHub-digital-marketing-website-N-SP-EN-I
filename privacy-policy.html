Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you access our digital marketing training services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your data is used responsibly and transparently. Please read this policy carefully to understand how your information is handled.

I. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information to provide and enhance your experience with our digital marketing training programs.

1.1.1 Information You Provide

When you register for our training or contact us, we may ask for personal data that can identify or contact you. This may include:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Mailing Address
  • Course Preferences and Learning History

1.1.2 Automatically Collected Data

We may collect technical data about how you access and use our training platform. This may include your browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, IP address, and similar diagnostic data to help us improve our training experience.

1.1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your training preferences and monitor the effectiveness of our learning tools. Cookies are small files placed on your device that help enhance functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but disabling them may limit your access to parts of our services.

II. Disclosure of Personal Information

We may share your personal data only when necessary, including:

  • To comply with applicable laws or legal processes.
  • To maintain the integrity and security of our training systems.
  • To enforce our terms related to the use of digital marketing training materials.

III. Use of Third-Party Services

We may engage third-party service providers to help deliver or analyze our training content. These providers may access your data only to perform services on our behalf and are obligated not to use it for any other purpose.

IV. Google Ads

We may use Google Ads to promote our digital marketing training. Google uses cookies to display ads based on your interactions with our website. You can adjust your ad preferences by visiting the Google Ad Settings page.

V. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us via the details below. We will respond promptly in accordance with data protection standards.

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be published on this page. Continued use of our training services after changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
